2. Description of Service

Sikani POS is a scan-to-pay smart point-of-sale system that enables seamless digital payments between buyers and sellers. The Service consists of:

  • Sikani Buyer App: a mobile application that allows consumers to scan QR codes, make payments using card, mobile money, or wallet balance, view transaction history, and manage their account
  • Sikani Seller App: a mobile application that allows merchants to generate dynamic QR codes, receive payments, manage their product catalog, track sales analytics, process refunds, and manage their business operations
  • Payment processing: facilitation of real-time payment transactions between buyers and sellers through integration with a third-party payment processor
  • Wallet system: a digital wallet that allows users to store funds, make payments, and receive settlements
  • Web dashboard: optional web-based tools for sellers to manage their business operations

  • Wallet funds may only be used for transactions within the Sikani platform
