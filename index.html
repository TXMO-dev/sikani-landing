How It Works

Three steps.
Zero friction.

1

Seller creates a payment

Enter amount, generate QR code. The request includes amount, merchant name, and a 60-second expiry timer.

2

Buyer scans and pays

Open the Sikani app, scan the QR code. Choose card, MoMo, or wallet. Confirm payment.

3

Both get notified

Instant confirmation for both parties. Email receipt, SMS, push notification. Funds settle T+1.
